Add shared i18n helpers and align the three major extensions around the same
`locale`, `messages`, and `ui` configuration surface.

This release also localizes built-in labels for Simplified Chinese, Traditional
Chinese, English, and Japanese; fixes the code-block extension's inherited
defaults; and documents the new configuration conventions for consumers.
